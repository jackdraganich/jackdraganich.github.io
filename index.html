About Us

Welcome to Melbourne City Dye Works, where you can find exquisitely created yarns by hand. Each skein is painstakingly made in Australia with a love of warmth and creativity. Every thread is filled with affection and attention to detail thanks to our handcrafted process. We take pride in using the highest-quality products from trustworthy vendors who support sustainability. Our wide selection, which includes vivid cotton mixes and delicate merino wool, may be customised to fit any project or personal style. At Melbourne City Dye Works, our commitment to quality extends beyond the finished product. We appreciate the fun of making yarn and offer timely, attentive support to encourage your creative endeavours. By selecting us, you assist regional artisans and support Australia's growing yarn industry. Discover the joy of working with handcrafted Australian yarns. Learn about Melbourne City Dye Works and let your imagination soar.
